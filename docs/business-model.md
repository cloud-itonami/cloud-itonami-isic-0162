# Business Model: Community Agronomy Support

## Classification
- Repository: `cloud-itonami-0162`
- ISIC Rev.5: `0162` — agronomy support — soil and crop scouting, advisory, and targeted treatment for smallholders
- Social impact: food-security rural-livelihoods environment

## Customer
- smallholders, cooperatives and extension programs that cannot accept closed agronomy SaaS

## Offer
- field sampling and lab integration, crop scouting, advisory workflows, treatment records, soil and yield audit

## Revenue
- setup fee per farm, monthly monitoring subscription, treatment support, integration services

## Trust Controls
- treatment records cannot be suppressed; advisory must cite evidence; field operations stay outside LLM control
- a robot action the governor refuses is never dispatched to hardware
- every dispatch, hold, approval and disclosure path is auditable
- sensitive operating and personal data stays outside Git
